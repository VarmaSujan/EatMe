Our Story

Eat Me is a small family run confectionery established in the 50's by our grand mother Suzie. We are built on tradition, passed down recipes and a sense of old school quality. We'd like to think that we bake it like they used to make it. With years of tradition, guidance and practice we have come to be known for our quality and creativity in baking. We specialise in custom wedding cakes and our neighbourhood can't resist our cupcakes. I've spent most of my life at Eat Me and am happy to carry our traditions and conectionaries into another genration with the support of you. Regards Katie
